# Post-Generation Checklist — 4 Steps Every Time

Do these 4 things after generating any AI video. Takes 10 extra minutes. Makes a massive difference in whether content looks organic or AI-generated.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add 2–3% film grain in CapCut">
    Removes the clean digital look. This single step makes AI footage blend with real UGC in the feed.

    In CapCut: Effects → Overlay → Film Grain → set to 2–3%
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add background noise layer">
    Drop in coffee shop ambience, room tone, or street noise under your voiceover.

    AI voices sound fake partly because the audio is too clean. Room noise makes it feel real.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export at 1080p not 4K">
    Platform compression handles the rest. Lower resolution actually blends better with real UGC.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run through compression">
    Upload to Telegram, download, re-upload to your platform.

    This adds natural compression artifacts that make content feel more authentic.
  </Step>
</Steps>
