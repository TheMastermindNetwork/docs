# Pacing Rules

- **Cut the first 3 seconds faster than your instinct says** — algorithms reward early retention above everything else
- **Cut every 2–3 seconds maximum** during the build section
- **No lingering shots** — if you feel like you should cut, you should have already cut
- **Layer captions** — most viewers watch without sound

## The retention curve

TikTok and Meta algorithms measure:
1. Whether people watch past 3 seconds
2. Whether people watch past 50%
3. Whether people watch to the end or replay

The first cut determines everything. If you lose someone in the first 3 seconds, the rest of your video doesn't matter.

Cut faster. Then cut faster again.
