# The Test Velocity Advantage

<Info>
  Source: [https://x.com/knoxtwts/status/2029207500621332685](https://x.com/knoxtwts/status/2029207500621332685)
</Info>

## The core insight most people miss

The advantage isn't cheaper videos. It's the ability to **test more**.

When one UGC video costs $400 you test 3 and hope one works. When one costs $2 you test 30 and statistics start working in your favor.

## The numbers

| Approach | Budget | Videos | Expected winners |
|----------|--------|--------|-----------------|
| Traditional UGC | $5,000 | 10–12 | 2–3 |
| AI UGC | $2,000 | 40–60 | 5–8 |

The brand spending $5k on traditional UGC gets their creative for the month. The brand spending $2k on AI content is running 30+ tested creatives by week 3 — while their competitor is still waiting on their UGC creator to reshoot the video that flopped.

## How to use test velocity

1. Generate 20 videos with different hooks, different angles, different characters
2. Run all 20 with a small budget ($5–10/day each)
3. After 72 hours, identify the 3–5 winners (lowest CPM, highest CTR)
4. Generate 10 variations of each winner
5. Scale the winners, kill the rest

By week 2 you have statistically-validated creative. No guessing.
