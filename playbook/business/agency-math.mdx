## What a traditional creative team costs a client

| Role | Monthly cost |
|------|-------------|
| UGC creator on retainer | $2,000–4,000 |
| Videographer | $4,000–6,000 |
| Editor | $3,000–5,000 |
| Voiceover artist | $200–500 per video |
| Project manager | $3,500–5,000 |
| **Total** | **$12,700–20,500/month** |

Delivery time: 2–3 weeks per batch of 10–15 videos. Revision turnaround: 3–5 business days.

## What you charge

- **$5,000–8,000/month**
- 40–50 videos delivered
- Turnaround in days not weeks
- Revisions in hours not business days

## Your actual costs

~$50 per finished video at most. Margin: **80%+**.

The client saves $7,000–15,000/month and gets 3x the output.

## The pitch

The client isn't choosing between you and a cheaper option. They're choosing between you and paying $14k/month for slower output. Frame it that way.
