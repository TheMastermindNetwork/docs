# AI Content Page + Whop Product

## The model

Build an AI character (like the Rabbi or Rolls Royce personality accounts). Post value content in your niche. Build audience. Sell a Whop product (course, community, templates) to your following.

## The full stack

| Step | Tool |
|------|------|
| Create character | Nano Banana Pro |
| Make it talk | HeyGen or InfiniteTalk |
| Edit and assemble | CapCut |
| Monetize | Whop |

## Why this works

You're not selling yourself — you're selling a character. The character never burns out, never has a bad day, and looks exactly the same across every single video.

Once the character is built, you have a consistent brand identity that scales as fast as you can produce content.

## What to sell on Whop

- Templates (hook templates, script templates, JSON prompts)
- A community where you share weekly winning content formats
- A mini-course on the pipeline
- 1:1 reviews of their content

Start with templates — lowest friction, easiest to deliver.
