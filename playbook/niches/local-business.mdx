# Local Business Ad Agency — OpenClaw + Arcads

<Info>
  Source: [https://x.com/knoxtwts/status/2029575323004637474](https://x.com/knoxtwts/status/2029575323004637474)
</Info>

## The play

OpenClaw scrapes every medspa, dentist, personal trainer, and real estate agent in any city using Google Maps + Apify. It analyzes their current content and identifies who is posting low-quality ads. Then Arcads generates a better ad for that business automatically. OpenClaw sends a cold DM with the finished video attached.

## Why the close rate is high

You show up with a completed ad already made. The business owner watches it and goes *"wait, you already made this?"* — that's the close.

## The economics

| Item | Cost |
|------|------|
| Production cost per video | ~$50 |
| What local businesses pay/month | $500–2,000 |
| 20 videos in tool costs | ~$1,000 |
| What you charge | $5,000+ |

## Getting started

1. Set up OpenClaw and connect Google Maps + Apify
2. Pick one city and one vertical (start with medspas or gyms — high budget, obvious ROI)
3. Scrape 50–100 businesses
4. Generate a sample ad for the 5 worst-looking ones using Arcads
5. Send cold outreach with the video attached
