# Note-Taking App — 6 Million Views

<Info>
  Source: [https://x.com/onlinedopamine/status/2025956313625137238](https://x.com/onlinedopamine/status/2025956313625137238)
</Info>

## The format

AI image slideshows with text overlays pushing a story. In this case: a glow-up after a divorce. The emotional narrative drives the content. The product (note-taking app) is attached to the transformation story.

## The lesson

Your product doesn't need to be the star. The story is the star. The product is the thing that makes the story possible.

## AI slideshow tools

- InVideo AI
- Pictory
- CapCut AI
- BigMotion AI

These take your product images and lifestyle photos and assemble them into short-form video with transitions, text pops, and trending audio.
