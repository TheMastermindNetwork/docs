## What is an AI slideshow?

A 15–30 second short-form video made from still images — product shots, lifestyle photos, text overlays — with transitions, zoom effects, and trending audio. No filming. No actors. No production.

## Why it performs

A slideshow with a strong text hook doesn't require the viewer to make a social judgment about whether they like or trust the person on screen. They just read the first text element and decide if it's relevant.

Less friction = higher watch-through = more algorithmic distribution.

## Content categories that convert hardest

| Category | Angle |
|----------|-------|
| **Pain amplification** | Opens with exact pain point, walks through consequences, soft product intro |
| **Benefit demonstration** | Leads with the outcome/transformation, builds the case |
| **Social proof cascade** | Rapid-fire testimonials, star ratings, sales volume indicators |
| **Urgency framing** | "Price increases Friday" not "Limited time offer" — specific beats generic |

## Best products for this format

- Fitness/wellness with visual transformation
- Beauty technology
- AI productivity tools
- Dating/relationship apps
- Dropship gadgets with novelty appeal
