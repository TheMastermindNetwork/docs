# Tooth/Dental Niche — Fear-Based Content

<Info>
  Source: [https://x.com/knoxtwts/status/2028586059777347650](https://x.com/knoxtwts/status/2028586059777347650)
</Info>

## The format

AI-generated videos of dental horror scenarios (swollen faces, tooth decay) with text overlay telling a story about *"skimping on toothpaste."* Millions of views. Then a comment planted in the replies recommending a specific toothpaste brand.

## Why it works

Fear content bypasses rational decision-making. A 15-second video about tooth decay and the viewer immediately thinks *"that could be me."* They scroll to comments looking for a solution — the product recommendation is waiting.

## The psychological mechanism

Manufactured urgency through pain. Works in any health/hygiene category where you can demonstrate consequences.

## Applicable to

- Skincare (aging, damage, acne scars)
- Supplements (deficiency consequences)
- Hair loss (progressive balding)
- Gut health (bloating, inflammation)

Anything where the fear of a bad outcome can be visualized.
