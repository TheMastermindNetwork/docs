# VSL B-Roll in Under 2 Hours

<Info>
  Source: [https://x.com/Aducate/status/2028894887844671618](https://x.com/Aducate/status/2028894887844671618)
</Info>

**What is b-roll?** B-roll is the visual footage that plays while someone is talking — product shots, lifestyle scenes, before/after visuals. In a VSL (video sales letter), it makes the video feel cinematic instead of just a talking head.

## The 7-step process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Script breakdown">
    Go through your script line by line. Mark which sections need pain visuals, solution visuals, and authority visuals (doctors, labs, results).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate character">
    Generate your character in Nano Banana Pro and save the reference image.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write scene prompts">
    Write a detailed prompt for each scene. Pain scenes: person grimacing, struggling. Solution scenes: relief, ease, happiness.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate in batches">
    - **Kling 2.6** for standard lifestyle shots
    - **Veo 3.1** for dynamic movement (liquid pouring, bottles shaking)
    - **Sora 2** for talking head segments

    Make 3–4 variations of each scene.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Color corrections">
    Use ChatGPT to generate JSON parameters for any color corrections or adjustments needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit with rhythm">
    Cut every 2–3 seconds maximum. No lingering shots.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Color grade everything">
    AI footage from different tools has different color profiles. Apply one LUT across all clips.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Check>
  Total time: **90 minutes** once you've done it a few times
</Check>
