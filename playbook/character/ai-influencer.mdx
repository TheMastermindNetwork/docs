## The iPhone Aesthetic principle

All AI influencer content should simulate high-end mobile photography, not professional camera work. Content that looks like an iPhone feels authentic. Content that looks like a studio shoot feels like an ad.

Key iPhone characteristics to always include:

| Setting | Value |
|---------|-------|
| Focal length | 24mm (main), 13mm (ultra wide), or 77mm (telephoto) |
| Color science | Apple ProRAW |
| Processing | Sharp details from Deep Fusion |
| Noise | Slight digital noise (not film grain) |
| Highlights | Slight blown-out (common in mobile) |

## The workflow

1. Search Pinterest for the type of influencer you want — *"lifestyle influencer bedroom"*, *"gym girl morning routine"*
2. Feed the image into your [Vision2JSON](/playbook/character/vision2json) Gemini Gem
3. Get the JSON back
4. Paste into Nano Banana 2
5. Generate your character

## Match demographics to your product every time

Selling supplements for women 50+? Generate a 55-year-old woman in a kitchen. Fitness supplement? Gym guy, 30, slightly sweaty, locker room background.

The character should look like the target customer, not a model. Aspiration is fine — but the viewer needs to see themselves in the character, not someone they'll never be.
