# Seedance 2.0 — Copy a Winning Ad's Motion

<Info>
  Source: [https://x.com/ahmad_a_wahabb/status/2026751759004348613](https://x.com/ahmad_a_wahabb/status/2026751759004348613)
</Info>

You upload a winning competitor video as a motion reference, your character image, and your product image. Seedance copies the camera movements, pacing, and shot style from the original video — but uses your character and product.

**Why this is powerful:** You're not guessing what works. You're taking proven cinematography from a video that's already converting and applying it to your assets.

## Setup

1. Upload original video as `@Video1`
2. Your character as `@Image1`
3. Your product as `@Image2`
4. Add your prompt describing any modifications
5. Generate

## When to use it

When you've found a competitor ad running for **60+ days** — meaning it's profitable — and want to make your version without starting from scratch.

<Tip>
  Seedance is available inside CapCut. You don't need a separate account.
</Tip>
