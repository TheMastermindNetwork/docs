# Product-in-Hand Shots

<Info>
  Source: [https://x.com/maxxmalist/status/2029262055555969297](https://x.com/maxxmalist/status/2029262055555969297)
</Info>

## The problem

Ask AI to generate *"a woman holding a serum bottle"* and you get a generic hand with something vaguely bottle-shaped that looks nothing like your product.

## The fix

Upload your character reference image **AND** a clean product photo together in Nano Banana. Then prompt the exact interaction:

```
girl holding [product name] in right hand, looking at camera, soft natural light, bathroom background
```

The model uses both images as anchors — your product stays recognizable.

## To animate it

Take that generated still image into Kling or Veo image-to-video mode. Upload the still as your starting frame. Prompt the motion:

```
hand lifts bottle slightly, she examines it, natural arm movement, slight handheld sway
```

## Tool choice for product shots

| Tool | Best for |
|------|----------|
| Kling | Better physical realism and more control |
| Veo | Better if you want ambient sound or dialogue in the clip |
| Sora | Most realistic output, best for hero shots |
