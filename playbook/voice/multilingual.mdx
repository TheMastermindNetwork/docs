# Multilingual Content at Scale

ElevenLabs can generate your cloned voice speaking Spanish, Portuguese, Arabic, French — any major language — with natural pronunciation. Same voice identity, different language.

## How to use it

1. Clone your base voice in ElevenLabs
2. Write your script in the target language (use Claude to translate)
3. Generate with your cloned voice — ElevenLabs handles the pronunciation automatically

## When this matters

If you're selling a product that ships internationally or running ads in multiple markets, this is one of the most underused capabilities in the stack.

Generate the same script in 5 languages in one session. Same character, same voice identity, 5x the addressable audience with no extra production work.
